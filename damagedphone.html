Questions and Answers

Q: What phones can you install a screen protector on?

A: Any phone given we have the screen protector in stock or brought in by the customer.

Q: What if I don't know what is wrong with my phone.

A: We always require a diagnostic to be included with any unknown issues.

Q: What if I forgot my password and my phone is locked?

A: Without a password our only option is to restore the phone. However, you may find assistance with your online passwords through your device's manufacture.
